# q17_已知函数 f( xrigh

## 题目

已知函数 $f\left( x\right)  = \sin x$ .
(1)求函数 $y = {2f}\left( x\right)  \cdot  f\left( {x + \frac{\pi }{2}}\right)  + 1$ 的单调递减区间；
(2)求函数 $g\left( x\right)  = 2{f}^{2}\left( x\right)  + f\left( {\frac{\pi }{2} - x}\right)  + 2, x \in  \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack$ 的值域.

## 解析

(1) $y = {2f}\left( x\right)  \cdot  f\left( {x + \frac{\pi }{2}}\right)  + 1 = 2\sin x\cos x + 1 = \sin {2x} + 1$ .
令 ${2k\pi } + \frac{\pi }{2} \leq  {2x} \leq  {2k\pi } + \frac{3\pi }{2}, k \in  Z$ ,得 ${k\pi } + \frac{\pi }{4} \leq  x \leq  {k\pi } + \frac{3\pi }{4}, k \in  Z$ ,
所以 $y = {2f}\left( x\right)  \cdot  f\left( {x + \frac{\pi }{2}}\right)  + 1$ 的单调递减区间是 $\left\lbrack  {{k\pi } + \frac{\pi }{4},{k\pi } + \frac{3\pi }{4}}\right\rbrack  , k \in  Z$ .
(2) $g\left( x\right)  = 2{\sin }^{2}x + \cos x + 2 =  - 2{\cos }^{2}x + \cos x + 4$ .
令 $t = \cos x$ ,因为 $x \in  \left\lbrack  {0,\frac{\pi }{2}}\right\rbrack$ ,所以 $t \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$ .
此时函数 $g\left( x\right)$ 转化为 $y =  - 2{t}^{2} + t + 4, t \in  \left\lbrack  {0,1}\right\rbrack$ .
对于二次函数 $y =  - 2{t}^{2} + t + 4$ ，其二次项系数 $a =  - 2 < 0$ ，图象开口向下，
对称轴为 $t =  - \frac{b}{2a} =  - \frac{1}{2 \times  \left( {-2}\right) } = \frac{1}{4}$ ,
当 $t = \frac{1}{4}$ 时, $y =  - 2 \times  {\left( \frac{1}{4}\right) }^{2} + \frac{1}{4} + 4 =  - 2 \times  \frac{1}{16} + \frac{1}{4} + 4 = \frac{33}{8}$ ,
当 $t = 1$ 时， $y =  - 2 \times  {1}^{2} + 1 + 4 =  - 2 + 1 + 4 = 3$ ，
当 $t = 0$ 时, $y =  - 2 \times  {0}^{2} + 0 + 4 = 4$ ,
所以值域为 $\left\lbrack  {3,\frac{33}{8}}\right\rbrack$ .
