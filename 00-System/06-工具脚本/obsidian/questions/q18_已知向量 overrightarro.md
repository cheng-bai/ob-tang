# q18_已知向量 overrightarro

## 题目

已知向量 $\overrightarrow{a}$ 、 $\overrightarrow{b}$ 满足 $\left| \overrightarrow{a}\right|  = \sqrt{3}$ ， $\left| \overrightarrow{b}\right|  = 1$ ，设 $\overrightarrow{a}$ 与 $\overrightarrow{b}$ 的夹角为 $\theta$ .
(1)当 $\theta  = \frac{\pi }{6}$ ，求 $\overrightarrow{a}$ 与 $\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}$ 的夹角；
(2)若对任意实数 $x$ ，不等式 $\left| {\overrightarrow{a} + x\overrightarrow{b}}\right|  \geq  \left| {\overrightarrow{a} + \overrightarrow{b}}\right|$ 恒成立，求 $\cos \theta$ 的值.

## 解析

(1) $\cos \theta  = \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}$ ,
则 $\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \left( {\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}}\right)  = {\overrightarrow{a}}^{2} + 2\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} = {\overrightarrow{a}}^{2} + 2\left| \overrightarrow{a}\right| \left| \overrightarrow{b}\right| \cos \theta  = 3 + 2\sqrt{3} \times  1 \times  \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} = 6$ ,
${\left| \overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}\right| }^{2} = {\overrightarrow{a}}^{2} + 4\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} + 4{\overrightarrow{b}}^{2} = 3 + 4 \times  \sqrt{3} \times  1 \times  \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} + 4 = {13},$
则 $\left| {\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}}\right|  = \sqrt{13}$ ，
则 $\cos  < \overrightarrow{a},\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b} >  = \frac{\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \left( {\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}}\right) }{\left| \overrightarrow{a}\right|  \cdot  \left| {\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}}\right| } = \frac{6}{\sqrt{3} \times  \sqrt{13}} = \frac{2\sqrt{39}}{13}$ ,
故 $\overrightarrow{a}$ 与 $\overrightarrow{a} + 2\overrightarrow{b}$ 夹角为 $\arccos \frac{2\sqrt{39}}{13}$ .
(2)不等式 $\left| {\overrightarrow{a} + x\overrightarrow{b}}\right|  \geq  \left| {\overrightarrow{a} + \overrightarrow{b}}\right|$ 恒成立，
得 ${\overrightarrow{a}}^{2} + {x}^{2}{\overrightarrow{b}}^{2} + {2x}\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} \geq  {\overrightarrow{a}}^{2} + {\overrightarrow{b}}^{2} + 2\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b}$ ,
即 ${x}^{2}{\overrightarrow{b}}^{2} + {2x}\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} - {\overrightarrow{b}}^{2} - 2\overrightarrow{a} \cdot  \overrightarrow{b} \geq  0$ 恒成立,
则 ${x}^{2} + 2\sqrt{3}x\cos \theta  - 1 - 2\sqrt{3}\cos \theta  \geq  0$ 恒成立,
所以 $\Delta  = {\left( 2\sqrt{3}\cos \theta \right) }^{2} - 4\left( {-1 - 2\sqrt{3}\cos \theta }\right)  \leq  0$ ,即 ${\left( \cos \theta  + \frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}\right) }^{2} \leq  0$ ,
解得 $\cos \theta  =  - \frac{\sqrt{3}}{3}$ .
